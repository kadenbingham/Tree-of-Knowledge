    Why trace word origins?

    Every word carries centuries of human thought compressed into syllables. Forest whispers of the Latin foris — "outside the walls". Disaster echoes the belief that stars guided fate. Etymology is archaeology with language as the dig site.

    EtymRoot gives you a guided journey through layers of meaning: the modern definition, the Latin or Greek root, the Proto-Indo-European ancestor, and the cultural story in between.
